About Me

I am a mechanical engineer with a strong background in manufacturing, supply chain, and sales, with more than 13 years of professional experience. I have held several roles at Military Industries, including R&D and Technical Engineering, Welding Engineering, and Supply Chain Management. My experience also includes positions at Manufacturing and Production as well Sales of HVAC Systems. This diverse experience demonstrates a comprehensive understanding of different facets of a business, from production to customer-facing roles..

I possess significant technical and operational expertise. I have managed technology transfers for military truck manufacturing and co-developed manufacturing processes. In my role as a Manufacturing Manager, I was in charge of various operations, including laser cutting, bending, CNC machining, and welding. A key achievement was managing to get American Welding Society (AWS) certification for factory welders. I am also experienced in implementing and using Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software, specifically SAP S4/Hana, for tasks like managing supply chain modules, sales, and marketing.

Finally, My skills extend beyond technical knowledge to include strong leadership, teamwork, and management abilities. I have experience developing KPIs, managing projects using MS Projects, and participating in internal audits for ISO 9001 Quality Management Systems. I also have conducted training programs and participated in both local and international exhibitions. My educational background includes a BSc in Mechanical Engineering Technology and an Associate's degree in Mechanical Maintenance Engineering Technology from Yanbu Industrial College.
